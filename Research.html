Research Areas

  • Protein Folding: Exploring quantum computing applications in understanding protein folding processes.

    Investigating quantum computing applications in understanding the complex process of protein folding. The goal is to leverage quantum algorithms to simulate and predict protein structures more efficiently than classical methods, which could have significant implications for drug discovery and bioinformatics.

  • mRNA Codon Optimization: Utilizing quantum algorithms for designing and optimizing mRNA sequences.

    Applying quantum algorithms for the design and optimization of mRNA sequences. This research aims to explore quantum computing's potential to enhance the efficiency of codon selection, ultimately improving the expression of desired proteins in synthetic biology and vaccine development.

  • mRNA Folding: Exploring quantum algorithms for opimizing the secondary structure of mRNA.

    mRNA Folding: Applying quantum computing techniques to study the complex folding patterns of mRNA molecules. The goal is to gain insights into the folding dynamics, leading to improved understanding of gene expression and potential applications in mRNA-based therapeutics.
